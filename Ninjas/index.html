Unicorns VS. Ninjas

Style

With a shimmering white coat, unicorns are known to dazzle. Ninjas are exact opposite, but don't think their black clothes are a fashion mistake. They use them to stay out of sight in bushes, shadows, and moonlight nights.

Winner: Unicorns outshine the competition

Tactics

Ninjas are tactical experts. Before you've realized a ninja attack is underway, they will sneak into your house, disable your alarms, and eat your cookies. And the while, the unicorns are still wandering somewhere in a meadow.

Winner: Never turn your back on a ninja

Weaponry

Ninjas are experts in hand-to-hand combat and masters of many exotic weapons, including the ninjato and katana (ninja swords), shuriken (throwing stars), and yumi (bow). But unicorns are tough competitors, with an unbreakable horn that never gets misplaced.

Winner: Tie
